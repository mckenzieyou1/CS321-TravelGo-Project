Welcome To TravelGo!!

To use this app, simply click on the location that you would like to visit. You will then select the max price you want to pay, the farthest you are willing to travel, and the address of where you are currently at. The app will then take your preferences and location into account, search its database for the best location to send you to, and output a hidden gem tailored to your desires. Have fun and as always, TravelGo has your back!
